Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The Approved Contractor Program recognizes qualified commercial roofing contractors who meet our standards for licensing, insurance, experience, and reputation. Approved contractors receive a badge to display on their website that links back to Commercial Roof Guide, creating a mutually beneficial relationship.

The Approved Contractor Program is currently free for qualified contractors. We may introduce premium tiers in the future with enhanced features, but the base listing and badge will remain available at no cost.

Contractors must hold a valid roofing license, carry general liability and workers' compensation insurance, have at least 3 years of commercial roofing experience, and maintain a positive online reputation with no unresolved complaints.

The badge embed code includes a dofollow link back to Commercial Roof Guide. When placed on your website, it creates a backlink relationship. Backlinks from authoritative, niche-relevant websites are a significant ranking factor in Google's algorithm. The topical relevance of a commercial roofing resource linking to a commercial roofing contractor carries strong SEO value.

The badge design is standardized to maintain program credibility and recognition. You can adjust the display size using the width and height attributes in the embed code, but the badge image itself should not be altered. Consistent branding ensures the badge is recognizable and trustworthy.

Most applications are reviewed within 5-7 business days. We verify licensing, insurance, and reputation before approving contractors. You will receive an email notification once your application has been reviewed with next steps.
