Codes & Energy

Building Code and Energy Requirements in Maine

Commercial Code Framework

  • Commercial roofing projects generally follow adopted IBC-based code editions with state and local amendments.
  • Permit reviews typically require assembly details, fire classification, drainage approach, and wind-uplift attachment data.
  • Re-cover limitations and fire/wind classifications are enforced through local building departments.
  • Submittals often include manufacturer system data and inspection closeout documentation.

Energy and Risk Requirements

  • Energy compliance commonly uses adopted IECC pathways or ASHRAE 90.1 alternatives based on jurisdiction.
  • Insulation upgrades are frequently triggered during full replacement scopes.
  • Insurers often expect tested assemblies and documented maintenance for larger assets.
  • NDL warranty strategies and periodic inspection records are central to long-term risk control.
