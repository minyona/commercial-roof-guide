About Commercial Roof Guide

An independent educational resource helping commercial building owners and facility managers make informed roofing decisions -- without sales pressure.

Our Mission

Independent. Unbiased. Free.

Commercial Roof Guide exists for one reason: to give building owners and facility managers the information they need to make smart roofing decisions. We are not a roofing contractor. We are not affiliated with any manufacturer. We do not sell roofing materials or installation services. Every guide, calculator, and tool on this site is built to educate -- not to sell.

Transparency

How We Make Money

Contractor Referral Fees

We connect building owners with pre-screened commercial roofing contractors through our free estimate form. When a contractor receives a lead through our platform, we earn a referral fee. This allows us to keep all guides and tools completely free. Our editorial content is never influenced by contractor relationships. We recommend roofing systems based on data, building science, and industry standards -- never based on who pays us.
